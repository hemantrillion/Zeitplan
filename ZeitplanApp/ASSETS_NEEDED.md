# Assets You Need to Add

Place these files in the `assets/` folder before building:

| File | Source | Size / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| `assets/icon.png` | Your ZIETPLAN.png (square version) | 1024×1024 px |
| `assets/adaptive-icon.png` | Same as icon.png | 1024×1024 px |
| `assets/splash.png` | Your ZIETPLAN.png (the square logo) | 1242×2436 px (centered on white) |
| `assets/splash_logo.png` | Your ZIETPLAN.png | 512×512 px works fine |
| `assets/logo_dark.png` | Your BLACK_PLAN.png | 1400×400 (use as-is) |
| `assets/logo_light.png` | Your WHITEPAN.png | 1400×400 (use as-is) |

## Quick Instructions
1. Rename your ZIETPLAN.png → `icon.png`, copy to `assets/`
2. Copy same file as `adaptive-icon.png`
3. For the splash: use your square logo on a white background saved as `splash.png`
4. Copy your square logo as `splash_logo.png`
5. Rename BLACK_PLAN.png → `logo_dark.png`
6. Rename WHITEPAN.png → `logo_light.png`
